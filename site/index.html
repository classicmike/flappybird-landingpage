About

Ever wanted to play with Flappy Birds Again? Now you can!

The original highly addictive iOs game by Dong Nguyen in 2013, was a very popular game loved by millions. Unfortunately due to its highly addictive nature, Nguyen pulled it from the app store, resulting in people creating clones of the game and ripping it off at ridiculous prices! :( .... Until now!

Introducing Flappy Bird Remastered, a remake of the original game available free for you to play in the comfort of your browser

Relive the excitement as you guide your little flappy friend through all of the obstables, guiding him to GLORY!
